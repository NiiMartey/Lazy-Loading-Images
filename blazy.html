Teen Titans Go!

Characters

Robin

Robin is the perfectionist leader of the group, whose main complaint is that the other Titans won't do what he says.

Starfire

Starfire was Koriand'r, an alien princess from the planet Tamaran who was next in line for the throne and rule as Queen.

Cyborg

Cyborg is a laid-back, half-teen, half-robot who's more interested in pizza and video games than in fighting crime.

Raven

Raven is a half-demon and half human who spent many years learning to control her powers and hide them from her evil father.

Beast Boy

Beast Boy is a lovable loafer who transforms into animals when he's not watching TV or playing video games.

Silkie

Silkie was created as Larva M3-19 by Killer Moth for use in an army of giant mutant moths to dominate Jump City.

Bumblebee

Bumblebee (a.k.a. Karen Beecher) is the leader of Titans East and appeared with her teammates at the annual Titans East party.

Aqualad

Aqualad is a hero in Teen Titans Go! who made his debut in Starliar. He is a member of the Titans East.

Speedy

Speedy (Roy Harper) is a hero character in Teen Titans Go! and a member of the Titans East as well as Robin's old rival.

Más

Más is the twin brother of Menos, forming Más y Menos of the Titans East. Mas and Menos are taken up as Robin's students.

Menos

Menos is the twin brother of Más, forming Más y Menos of the Titans East. Mas and Menos are taken up as Robin's students.

Batman

Batman (real name Bruce Wayne) is the superhero defender of Gotham City and Robin's adoptive father and mentor.

Batgirl

Batgirl, the daughter of Commissioner Gordon, began her career as a solo fighter who occasionally joined the Dynamic Duo.

The Wonder Twins

The Wonder Twins are superhero partners, Jayna and Zan, whose powers transform them into animals or forms of water matter.

Commissioner Gordon

Commissioner Gordon is minor character in Teen Titans Go! who made his debut in La Larva de Amor as a cameo.

Aquaman

Aquaman is half human and half Atlantean. He has the power of telepathy, and can communicate with the creatures of the sea.

Superman

Superman is the superhero defender of Metropolis, a member of the Justice League and the Legion of Superheroes.

Green Lantern

Green Lantern is a minor hero character in Teen Titans Go! who made a guest debut in "Real Boy Adventures".

What's this all about?

Teen Titans Go! features hilarious, all-new adventures of Robin, Cyborg, Starfire, Raven and Beast Boy. Sure, they're still superheroes who save the day, but what happens when they're done fighting crime? They love to have fun and goof off, but they also have to deal with the issues of being five teen roommates, whether that's chores and romantic tension or demon dad drop-ins and impromptu meatball parties. There's no shortage of pandemonium in the Tower as the Titans get up to super-powered hijinks and fantastic pranks. Join the Teen Titans and see what sort of comedy chaos their rivalries and relationships cause next.
